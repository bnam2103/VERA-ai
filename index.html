Built for real conversation

Real-time voice

Continuous listening, push-to-talk, or keyboard — switch modes to match your environment.

Interrupt anytime

Cut in mid-response to correct, redirect, or add context — like talking to a person, not a queue.

Work mode

Reasoning panels, checklists, and multi-step plans for focused tasks without losing voice control.

Actions & tools

Weather, news, timers, music, and more — voice-driven utility with dedicated panels when you need depth.

How it works

  1. Speak — your speech is recognized in real time.
  2. Reason — VERA routes intent to chat, work mode, or tools.
  3. Respond — hear synthesized speech or see structured results in-panel.
