The problem

AI assistants are powerful, but the interaction still feels like managing a chat box.

You type, wait, read, clarify, switch tools, and repeat. That works for deep research, but it creates friction when you just want to ask something quickly, stay focused, or keep working without stopping to prompt.

The solution

VERA makes the interaction voice-native.

Speak naturally, get real-time responses, and let focused tools appear when they are useful. Instead of working around a chatbot, VERA is designed to feel closer to an assistant that moves with your workflow.

How does VERA work?

Talk naturally and get spoken responses back in real time. Switch between continuous listening, push-to-talk, and keyboard mode depending on your environment.

Interrupt VERA mid-response to correct, redirect, or add context — closer to speaking with another person than a turn-based chat.

VERA uses context like habits and preferences to give more helpful responses. Personal data is planned to stay local on your device.

Answer utility questions like time, date, weather, news, and stock prices through voice — with dedicated panels for richer interactions like news and music.
